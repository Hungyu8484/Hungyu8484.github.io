Club Session List
  • Laser Diffraction & LDR Test
    Measuring interference patterns and light intensity to establish the LDR voltage-intensity relationship.
  • Planck's Constant via I–V Curves
    Using linear regression of LED threshold voltage vs. wavelength to estimate h (including error analysis).
  • Elasticity of Plastic Films
    Determining equivalent elastic constants through force/vibration methods, connecting stress-strain relationships.
  • Double Pendulum (Chaotic Pendulum)
    Video tracking of chaotic behavior, demonstrating high sensitivity to initial conditions.
  • Popsicle-Stick Chain Reaction
    Energy storage and instantaneous release; measuring wave front velocity and geometric influences.
  • Magnetic Hysteresis
    Observing B-H loops, remanence and coercivity, connecting to material properties.
  • Film Discussion: Interstellar
    From black holes to time dilation, discussing scientific basis and dramatic presentation.
